About Us

Chary Logistics was established in 2011 as a third party logistics (3PL) firm. We are a one stop shop for all your logistics outsourcing requirements. Based in Nairobi, we continue to scale and tailor our service to match market dynamics and client needs. We have the capacity to optimize your supply chain to minimize costs and wastes, and maximize returns. We guarantee to surpass your expectations as we meet the industry’s best practices. Our range of logistics services includes:

• Freight Forwarding

• Customs Clearance

• Warehousing

• Distribution
