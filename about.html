WHO WE ARE

Grip up -We are a registered non-profit organization based in United Kingdom. Since our inception we have focused our approach on Four thematic areas, they are water, Food, shelter, sanitation. we are providing support and resources for the essential components to life which is our main Agenda We aim to guarantee access to water and basic sanitation, which are both necessary for the life and development of communities. our other focus is good nutrition which is the foundation of human well-being and will contribute to improving problems associated with lack of food Whether people have sought protection and refuge in formal refugee camps or informal settlements, we provide temporary shelter, help repair damaged dwellings infrastructure, and reconstruct permanent homes. These initiatives enable vulnerable displaced populations to live in dignity and security The communities we help are in some of the hardest to reach and most fragile places. Places prone to conflict, natural disasters, climate change, flooding, and extreme hunger. We also know that real progress comes not just from reacting to shocks and disasters, that it happens first and foremost at the community level, when people are equipped and empowered to transform their own lives. We work with communities to build their resilience and ensure their transformation is sustainable and long-lasting.

OUR MISSION

Ensuring the Basic Needs (FOOD,WATER,SHELTER) are met For the vulnerable communities . To achieve this mission, we engage in long term development work, build resilience, respond to emergency situations, and seek to address the root causes of the problems through our development education and advocacy work.

Our vision

A world without hunger, where all have access to a decent standard of living and the opportunities and choices essential to a long, healthy and creative life; a world where everyone is treated with dignity and respect. ,

Our values

Which is Built on our voluntary, compassionate commitment We listen: Listening and partnership are key to empowering the poorest and most vulnerable to transform their own lives. We respond rapidly: People affected by disasters are entitled to have their most basic needs met through rapid, effective, and principled responses. We are committed: Going the extra mile to support communities in times of need and in the face of very difficult operating environments. We are innovative: Finding effective solutions requires innovative thinking combined with a pragmatic approach. We are accountable: Accountability and transparency are central to all of our actions and use of resources.
