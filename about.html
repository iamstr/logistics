litte bit about us

As Best freight forwarding company, Naif Travel & Logistics in Kenya is established in the biggest cities in the country. We offer among othersvalue-added clearing & forwarding, logistics & warehousing solutions and dedicated services for commodities across East Africa

With Kenya as the primary logistics center for the East African Region, Naif Travel & Logistics has invested in various hubs to support both local and international companies to power their supply chains.Our key strengths are in having a strong management team and well trained and motivated skilled workers. Being a medium sized company, we are able to provide our clients with quality, personalized, cost effective, efficient and reliable services.
